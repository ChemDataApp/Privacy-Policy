Data Deletion

If you wish to delete your ChemData account and associated data, follow these steps:

  1. Email us at chemdataapp@gmail.com.
  2. Use the subject line: "Account Deletion."
  3. Send the email from the address linked to your ChemData account.

We will process your request within 15 business days. During this time, your data will remain stored for legal and security purposes. After this period, all data will be permanently deleted.

Data Deleted:

  • Account information: Name, email, and profile photo.
  • Usage history and app preferences.

Data Retained:

  • Transaction records via Stripe for compliance with tax regulations.
